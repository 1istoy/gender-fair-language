🌈 Words Matter

Promoting Gender-Fair Language in the Filipino Community

Why Gender-Fair Language?

Gender-fair language promotes equality, respect, and inclusion. By choosing words that do not discriminate based on gender, we help build communities where everyone feels valued regardless of gender identity or expression.

Common Gender-Biased Terms and Inclusive Alternatives

Gender-Biased Gender-Fair Alternative
Ladies and Gentlemen Everyone / Distinguished Guests
Chairman Chairperson / Chair
Fireman Firefighter
Policeman Police Officer
Salesman Salesperson
Mankind Humankind / Humanity
"Panglalaki lang iyan." "Para sa kahit sino."
"Pambabae lang iyan." "Lahat ay maaaring gumawa nito."

Our Message

Ang paggamit ng gender-fair language ay nagpapakita ng paggalang, pagkakapantay-pantay, at pagiging inklusibo. Sa simpleng pagbabago ng ating mga salita, nakakatulong tayo sa pagbuo ng lipunang walang diskriminasyon.

Campaign Slogan
